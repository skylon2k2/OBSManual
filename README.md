# OBSManual
For Shareekatul Hussain Milton Facebook Live

<h1>How to get the Live Stream running on Facebook</h1>
<h2>Step 1</h2>
Open <b>OBS Studio

<h2>Step 2</h2></b>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/0596d52b-4dc7-473f-adb8-d27d2406f054" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Click **Apply** pop-up named **Missing Files**.

<h2>Step 3</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/c35e6f78-aa83-4d46-bc20-0781c9404c4f" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Click **Settings** on the panel named **Controls** on the bottom right.

<h2>Step 4</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/4ee97311-8b06-49e0-8af7-d3642994bafe" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Click **Stream** in the menu on the left.

<h2>Step 5</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/b3967796-2b5e-4513-9cfc-a56f38b6f5bf" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Open the **Shareekatul Hussain Milton** facebook page on any browser and click **Live Video**.

<h2>Step 6</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/8ce1c1f1-8e4a-4c4d-b6c3-74a060fe34d1" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Click the **Go Live** button.

<h2>Step 7</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/6fabf31c-87b1-4e8a-9f11-a58755109ea8" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Copy the **Stream Key** and paste into OBS Studio in the **Stream Key** field and click **Apply** then **OK**.

<h2>Step 8</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/2fafca50-0d4f-455f-8114-487965024803" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

In OBS Studio, click **Start Streaming**.

<h2>Step 9</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/9b77b9b2-0602-4ed6-b9c1-190e8dea03ca" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

On Facebook, make sure that both **Connect video source** and **Complete post details** are checked. There is an option to add a Title and Description under **Add post details**.

<h2>Step 10</h2>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/5ba9f002-8bd6-4009-b666-04aa46b63f63" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

Click **Go live** on Facebook. Make sure to click the play sign under **Video** in Facebook.


Now the live stream should be running.


<h1>How to end Live Stream on Facebook</h1>
<img src="https://github.com/skylon2k2/OBSManual/assets/90818966/527f8702-e7c0-4c82-93f5-8ce654cbe82d" alt="drawing" width="500"/>

In OBS Studio, click **Stop Streaming** loccated on the bottom right **Controls** panel.
